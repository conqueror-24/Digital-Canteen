Rating : 4/5

Sakshi

Rating : 3/5

"The food at the canteen is generally good, but there are days when it feels a bit hit-or-miss. Some dishes can be quite oily, and the wait times can get long during peak hours. That said, I do enjoy the variety and the occasional themed food days."
